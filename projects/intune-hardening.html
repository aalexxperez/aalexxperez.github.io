Intune Hardening & Compliance Baseline

Standardized device configuration and compliance using Intune policies and security baselines.

Overview

The goal was to improve security and consistency across endpoints. I created a baseline approach so new devices could be deployed fast while meeting compliance requirements.

Role: Intune / M365 Specialist Focus: Compliance + Hardening Tools: Intune, Entra ID

What I delivered

  • Clear policy structure (naming, scope, ownership).
  • Compliance policies and device configuration policies.
  • Security baselines and review process.
  • Reporting dashboards and simple documentation for admins.

Results

  • More consistent device posture across the fleet.
  • Faster onboarding for new devices and users.
  • Better visibility into compliance and drift.
